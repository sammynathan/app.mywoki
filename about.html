About Mywoki

Mywoki is a software platform designed to help startups, founders, and small teams manage business tools in a more unified and structured way.

History

Mywoki was founded in 2024 by Sammy Nathan. The platform was created in response to the growing complexity faced by startups using multiple disconnected software tools.

Product

The Mywoki platform provides a centralized interface where users can discover, activate, and manage third-party business software tools. It focuses on clarity, usability, and reducing operational friction.

Business Model

Mywoki operates on a subscription-based software-as-a-service (SaaS) model, offering both free and paid plans depending on usage and feature needs.

Availability

Mywoki is used by individuals and teams in multiple regions, including Kenya, Nigeria, the United States, Australia, and other international markets.
